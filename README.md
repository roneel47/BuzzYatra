# Buzzyatra - Your Smart Bengaluru Transit Companion

## Overview

Buzzyatra is a user-centric mobile application designed to revolutionize the public transportation experience in Bengaluru, India. It integrates intelligent route planning, proactive stop alerts, and an integrated emergency SOS system to make navigating the city's BMTC bus network more accessible, convenient, and safer for both new visitors and daily commuters.

## Features

-   **Intelligent Route and Fare Discovery:**
    -   Effortlessly find optimal BMTC bus routes between any two locations in Bengaluru.
    -   View estimated travel times, number of transfers, and route details on a map.
    -   Accurate fare calculation for suggested routes.
    -   *(Future Enhancement)* Real-time bus tracking to see the current location and estimated arrival time of buses.

-   **Proactive Stop Alert System:**
    -   Set your desired destination bus stop within the app.
    -   Receive timely audio, vibration, and/or visual alerts as you approach your stop (e.g., within 500 meters).
    -   Customizable alert preferences for distance and notification type.
    -   Seamless background operation ensures alerts even when the app is not in the foreground.

-   **Integrated Emergency SOS Functionality:**
    -   Configure a list of trusted emergency contacts.
    -   Quickly trigger an SOS alert with a prominent button.
    -   Instantly transmit your live GPS location to your emergency contacts.
    -   Option to include a customizable emergency message.
    -   *(Future Enhancement)* Potential integration with local emergency services.

## Proposed System

Buzzyatra aims to be more than just a navigation tool; it strives to be a reliable companion for public transport users in Bengaluru.

-   **For New Visitors and Tourists:** Simplifies navigating the BMTC network by providing clear route options and fare information.
-   **For Daily Commuters:** Enhances convenience by proactively alerting users of their approaching stops, minimizing missed destinations.
-   **For Everyone:** Prioritizes safety with an integrated SOS feature for quick assistance in emergencies.

## Drawbacks of the Existing System

Current public transport navigation and safety mechanisms in Bengaluru often suffer from:

-   Lack of integration between route planning, stop alerts, and emergency assistance.
-   Inconvenience for new users unfamiliar with the local bus network.
-   Risk of missing stops due to the absence of proactive alert systems.
-   Delayed emergency response due to manual communication and location sharing.
-   Limited transit-specific safety features in generic safety apps.

## Conclusion

Buzzyatra offers a streamlined mobile solution to enhance public transport in Bengaluru by integrating intelligent route discovery, proactive stop alerts, and a vital emergency SOS system. This application aims to improve convenience for all commuters, prevent missed stops for regular users, and provide a crucial safety net through instant location sharing in emergencies. By focusing on these core features, Buzzyatra strives to empower users with the necessary tools for a more efficient, convenient, and secure public transportation experience in Bengaluru, ultimately fostering greater ease and confidence in navigating the city's BMTC bus network.
